Core Innovation

Rethinking the Photoblog Frame

Most platforms just shrink a desktop website until it fits a smartphone, resulting in tiny navigation targets and broken immersion. Point treats your photo collection differently based on how you hold your device.

📱

On Mobile: The Touch-Native Stream

The border between posts and individual images is entirely blurred. Your collection becomes a single, continuous, tactile canvas guided by natural swipe physics. No clunky thumbnails to tap, no microscopic close buttons to hunt for.

🖥

On Desktop: The Classic Grid

A mouse is a precision tool, and Point respects that. Desktop visitors get a blazing-fast, organised masonry grid, intuitive hover states, and clear pagination. Familiar, powerful, and clean.

Feature Deep-Dive

Content is the UI. Metadata on Demand.

When you are viewing a photograph, nothing else should compete for your attention. Point eliminates layout clutter with an elegant overlay system.

✦

The Vanishing Act

Titles, descriptions, tags, and deep EXIF data float seamlessly over the image. They automatically fade away after a short timer or with a simple gesture — keeping your focus entirely on the art.

👆

Tap to Reveal

Want to know the focal length, the story behind the shot, or the location? A simple tap on mobile (or click on desktop) instantly brings the details back into view.

Built for Photographers

Retain 100% Visual Ownership

Point was built by a photographer, for photographers. Every decision respects your files, your audience, and your creative intent.

🖼

Zero Destructive Compression

Point respects your master files. Images are processed cleanly, retaining high fidelity across device sizes without aggressive artifacting.

📷

EXIF Integration

Native parsing of your camera data. Display camera models, apertures, shutter speeds, and ISO values beautifully without manual data entry.

🔒

Privacy-First Feed

No tracking pixels, no platform lock-in, no algorithmic manipulation. Share via standard RSS/Atom feeds so your audience follows on their own terms.

Built for Self-Hosters & Developers

Featherweight Infrastructure. Ultimate Control.

Point isn't bloated with resource-heavy machine learning models or massive database dependencies. It runs incredibly light — perfect for a home lab or a low-cost VPS.

🐳

Container-Native

Fully optimized for Docker and Podman. Drop it into any existing compose stack in under a minute.

📁

Declarative Control

Manage configurations and data paths safely using local-first volumes. Your data stays yours.

⚡

Lightning Performance

Minimal resource footprint, ultra-low memory usage, and instantaneous page generation.

compose.yml 
version: "3.8"

  point-photoblog:
    image: ghcr.io/darii/point:latest
    container_name: point_engine
    ports:
      - "8080:8080"
    volumes:
      - ./photos:/app/photos
      - ./config:/app/config
    restart: unless-stopped
